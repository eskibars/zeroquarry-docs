Account settings control collaboration, notifications, and report defaults for
your workspace.

![ZeroQuarry account overview with workspace settings and usage controls.](/img/screenshots/account-overview.png)

_The account overview is the control surface for notification defaults, team
management, model settings, and other workspace-wide preferences._

## Completion email

Enable scan completion email from **Account**. You can override the default on
individual scan forms.

Email notifications are best for long-running scans or Batch API artifact
generation.

## Slack notifications

Add an incoming Slack webhook under **Account** to post scan completion
summaries to a channel. When Slack is configured, scan forms can enable or
disable Slack posting for that scan.

Use a channel that matches the audience of the scan. For example, route CI scan
summaries to an engineering security channel, not a broad company channel.

## Team members

Plans that allow invitations can add teammates under **Account**. Roles are
account-scoped.

Use the smallest role that lets a teammate complete their work. Remove members
who no longer need access.

If you operate the platform as a super-admin, there is also a separate
account-creation invite flow on the platform admin dashboard. That flow sends
an activation link for a brand-new account on a selected tier. It is distinct
from teammate invitations inside an existing account.

## Preferences

Useful account preferences include:

- workflow mode for scan UX (`Guided scan`, `Security research`, or `AppSec triage`)
- low-confidence cutoff for exports
- whether to show the Disclosures shortcut
- scan completion email defaults
- report disclaimer overrides, when the plan allows report customization

These settings affect future interactions and exports. They do not rewrite
historical scan evidence.
